# About MERC

MERC, or Mechanical Engineering Robot Contest, is a robotics program hosted by C.A.T - an academic club of HCMUT's mechanical engineering department, composed of competitors from different universities in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. The event encourages STEM learning and cooperation by hosting competitions between robots in a yearly changed game.

In a typical game, two teams are labeled as different colors and they compete against each other for points. The team that has the most points wins the match.
